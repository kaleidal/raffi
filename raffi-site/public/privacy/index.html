Privacy Policy for Raffi

Last updated: February 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Raffi collects, uses, and stores information when you use the Raffi desktop app, mobile app, and website.

1. Who We Are

Raffi is a media application and related website available at raffi.al.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account data (if you sign in with Ave): user ID, email, profile name, avatar, and authentication token returned by Ave.
  • Cloud sync data: installed add-ons, watch progress, watch history visibility, lists and list items, and watch party state (for example playback time and play/pause state).
  • Local app data: preferences/settings, local cache, and locally stored session data on your device.
  • Downloads/local files: on mobile, downloaded media files and download metadata; on desktop, optional local library roots and indexed local media file paths used for local playback.
  • Diagnostic and product analytics (desktop): anonymous usage events through PostHog. Session replay is optional and off by default.
  • Technical logs (desktop): application logs that may include operational errors and system/runtime details.

3. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate your account and keep you signed in.
  • Sync your library, lists, and watch progress across devices.
  • Provide playback features like watch parties, downloads, and local file playback.
  • Maintain and improve app performance, reliability, and usability.
  • Deliver app updates and release information.

4. Third-Party Services

Raffi uses third-party services to provide core functionality:

  • Ave for account authentication.
  • Convex for synced app data storage.
  • PostHog for desktop analytics (with in-app controls).
  • GitHub Releases API for website/app release checks and download links.
  • Metadata/add-on endpoints (for example Cinemeta and third-party add-ons) for catalog and stream discovery.
  • YouTube (youtube-nocookie.com) for trailer embeds.
  • Discord RPC on desktop if you enable Discord rich presence.

These services may process network metadata such as IP address and request headers under their own privacy policies.

5. Torrent and Streaming Disclosure

If you use torrent-based sources, your IP address may be visible to peers in the torrent swarm. Raffi does not control third-party peer visibility or third-party add-on behavior.

6. Data Sharing

Raffi does not sell personal information. We share data with service providers only as needed to run the product features described in this policy.

7. Retention

  • Local device data remains until you clear it, sign out, or uninstall the app.
  • Synced cloud data is retained while your account is active or until deletion is requested.
  • Analytics retention is managed by the analytics provider configuration and controls.

8. Security

We use reasonable safeguards appropriate to the nature of the data. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Choices and Rights

  • Desktop analytics can be disabled in Raffi Settings.
  • Desktop session replay can be enabled/disabled separately in Settings.
  • You can clear watch history and local cached data from the app.
  • Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, or deletion of personal data.

10. Children's Privacy

Raffi is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by revising the "Last updated" date above.

12. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact: hello@kaleid.al
